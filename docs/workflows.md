# Workflow Templates

This document describes the early workflow ideas for AI Commerce Agent.

The project is designed to help users organize common cross-border commerce tasks into repeatable AI-assisted workflows.

## 1. Product Research Workflow

The product research workflow helps users collect and organize basic product information.

### Input

- Product category
- Target market
- Estimated price range
- Competitor examples
- Customer pain points
- Product advantages

### Output

- Product opportunity summary
- Target customer profile
- Market demand notes
- Competitor comparison
- Suggested positioning
- Content ideas

## 2. Supplier Matching Workflow

The supplier matching workflow helps users organize supplier information and compare potential partners.

### Input

- Product name
- Required quantity
- Target region
- Quality requirements
- Certification requirements
- Delivery timeline
- Budget range

### Output

- Supplier comparison table
- Key questions for suppliers
- Risk checklist
- Negotiation points
- Follow-up message draft

## 3. Content Generation Workflow

The content generation workflow helps users prepare product-related content.

### Input

- Product name
- Product features
- Target customer
- Selling points
- Platform or channel
- Tone of voice

### Output

- Product title
- Product description
- Short marketing copy
- Social media post
- Outreach email
- FAQ draft

## 4. Market Demand Analysis Workflow

The market demand analysis workflow helps users think through demand, competition, and feasibility.

### Input

- Region
- Product category
- Customer group
- Import/export considerations
- Local competitors
- Distribution method

### Output

- Demand summary
- Opportunity ranking
- Risk notes
- Entry strategy
- Suggested next steps
